High Rush

Level 1: Manual Drag Race

Hit the perfect RPM, shift clean, and outrun the CPU.

Controls

W Accelerate / Rev
S Brake
A D Steer L/R
E Nitrous
Left Shift Shift Up
Left Ctrl Shift Down
R Restart

Tip: During countdown, hold W and keep the RPM inside the green zone for a perfect launch.

How to Play

🟢 : Hold W to rev & Keep the RPM in the green zone during Countdown.

↑ Shifting: Press Left Shift when the needle hits the green zone.

🔥 Nitrous: Press E To boost.

💥 Dodge Traffic: Crashing will instantly kill your speed & momentum.

↓ Downshift: Press Left Ctrl to down shift when momentum is killed.
